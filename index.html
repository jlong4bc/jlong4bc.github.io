J Long: One of the Most Well-Known Athletes in Stoolaskii

J Long is celebrated worldwide for unforgettable runs in stoolaskii: a wild downhill sport where the rider sits on a barstool whose legs are buckled to skis, then carves through snow while wearing an unbuckled helmet. Equal parts daring and cartoon chaos, J Long helped make stoolaskii famous.
